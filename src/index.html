ABOUT ME

Currently, I am working as a purchasing specialist in the automotive industry. We produce extruded aluminum car roof rails for world-renowned brands. From purchasing side I am responsible for developing new projects, suppliers selection, realization of purchase requisitions for various production areas, looking for opportunities of savings and price negotiations. In addition, a large part of my work is based on Enterprise Resources Planning software like SAP.

I graduated from the Technical University of Lodz, engineer by education. The knowledge gained at the university taught me logical thinking, cooperation with others, not giving up, patience and many aspects of technical knowledge which I use cooperating with technical department and suppliers.

I have been interested in computers and technical innovations since an early age. More in hardware than software formerly. And a few years ago, I desired to try or create something new. I came across a product called Raspberry Pi. Small programmable computer. Then I thought - programming maybe? I bought a book by Michael Dawson, by which I had my first contact with Python programming language. Because of private and professional reasons the adventure did not last too long and I did not buy a little raspberry in the end. But I wrote some very simple working lines of code.

After several years of working in a corporation, I came to the point when I realized that it is not the place where I would like to spend the rest of my professional career. The idea of programming came back. I like to face of challenges and decided to become a programmer. I have a strong motivation to gain new knowledge and experience. In my opinion programming is based on logical thinking and requires constant searching for solutions which I personally like so much. Rapidly changing technology forces constant learning, requires self-development what gives a lot of satisfaction. Moreover, I am motivated by the possibility of remote working. Coding anywhere, exploring the world and meeting interesting people.

I chose front-end technologies and JavaScript language because I like creating and seeing immediate effects of my work. I have a fairly sense of aesthetics in my opinion and I like to take care of details. JavaScript is a versatile programming language and gives many opportunities for further development. It is usable on the front and backend. Despite complexity of JavaScript, it was a good choice. I see the capabilities to be a Fullstack Developer or take my chances at other technologies in the future.

Unfortunately, I currently have no commercial programming experience yet. However, I hope that will change soon. I am also aware of the current popularity of looking for a job in IT and number of applicants for Junior Developer. Nevertheless I am determined and highly motivated. I would really love to start working as programmer and have the opportunity to spend more time programming. I believe that someday my potential be noticed/appreciated and I will be able to spread my wings.
